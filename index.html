The Legend of Zendar

A Turkish Modification of Turkish game Mount&Blade for Crusader Kings II

View on GitHub

Warrider - 1 January, 1246​

January 1st, Morning. King Larec declared himself as the last Emperor's only and true heir. Meanwhile, former Swadian commander in Reyvadin become Tsar of Great Taiga. But he barely left his court. Larec sees that as a weakness and declares war to Yaroglek for the reunion of the Calradian Empire.

In the deserts, there is a united Sarrdakian Empire for many centuries. But there are religious conflicts between Shariz to Bariyye. With the death of mighty Sarrdakian Emperor, zealots sling mud at infidels because they poisoned the old Emperor.

Also, there are a lot of adventurers comes to independent and neutral town of Zendar to whom seeking their own fortune. Some of them sponsored by opportunist merchants to build their own party for Zendar as a mercenary army.

This is Warrider! 11 years before the Warband. So who you gonna be in this messed realm of Calradia?

Features​

  • A middle-age alternate history world in a alternate continent named Calradia besides Europa.
  • A huge map that contains whole continent of Calradia which is shown in Mount&Blade: Warband.
  • Total 765 provinces from basic 180 Mount&Blade: Warband territories as duchies.
  • There is no de jure empires at all. Build your own! And then you can reunite all over Calradian Empire.
  • 22 kingdoms from classic factions of Calradia.
  • Around 30 extended cultures for Calradian peoples with basic 6 factions of Calradia
  • Around 40 new religions of Calradian peoples. There are no atheist Calradians at all!
  • A whole new soundtrack including Mount&Blade and Warband musics!
  • And of course, we have Zendar!

FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions​

Who are you?​ We are a nice party with bunch of adventurers from origin of Mount&Blade series, Turkey. And of course we accept new ones from other factions.​

Crusader Kings II with Mount&Blade? Why?​ Because political and feudal structure is really fits perfect to Crusader Kings II. When I playing Mount&Blade, I really want to play as a map based strategy game. And of course, I want Bannerlord too. I mean... who doesn't :D?​

Is there a release date yet?​ No, not really. Actually we really want to release an open beta, or an early staged alpha. We completed tons of things and yet there is a lot of work to do.​
